Heroes Monument (Tugu Pahlawan)


Heroes Monument

Heroes Monument, also known as Heroic Monument (Indonesian:Tugu Pahlawan) is a monument in Surabaya, Indonesia. It is the main symbol of the city, dedicated to the people who died during the Battle of Surabaya on November 10, 1945. The 10 November Museum is located under the monument. Monument of Heroes, is a landmark monument in the city of Surabaya. This monument is 41.15 meters tall phallus-shaped or inverted spikes and is divided into 11 sections. To reach Heroes Monument, you will only need 15 minutes driving to the north from the hotel via Jalan Bubutan.

Ten November Museum

The Ten November Museum represents a memorialization of the happenings of the 10th November struggle. 10th November is celebrated as 'Heroes' Day' in Surabaya and is dedicated to the soldiers who gave up their lives for independence. The museum showcases weapons which were used in the Battle of Surabaya while also providing plenty of information about the history of the Indonesian freedom struggle. Located underneath the Heroes' Monument, the museum was built to explain the significance of the Heroes' Monument.

The museum and the Heroes' Monument is dedicated to the soldiers that lost their lives during the Battle of Surabaya. Soaring for over 40m into the sky, the venue becomes the site that hosts the processions that happen every 10th of November. The museum also contains photographs and dioramas that depict what happened on that fateful day.
